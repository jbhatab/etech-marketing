    Bin to Belt Sorting

    This customized solution is designed for configurations where sorting directly into a sling is not possible. It is also ideal for consolidating a large number of low-volume sorts into a smaller footprint. Linen is sorted into the single- or double-door continuous hoppers. When the hopper is full (as measured by piece counts or weight), the hoppers drop the sorted laundry onto a transfer conveyor where it is loaded into slings onto the monorail rail system. This is a highly effective automation option for a smaller space that processes many low-volume items.
